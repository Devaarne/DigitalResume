Devaarne Muller

I am a radical, queer, Feminist & Futurist visual and performance artist working under the name PUSSI POLITICA.

I focus on gender, politics and black identity in South Africa. I believe that South Africa needs deep healing from the trauma of colonisation and Apartheid.

I am interested in the transformative possibilities that developments in technology can bring marginalized South African communities, as well as what the intersection of art and technology can do to address inequality, improve community infrastructure and positively impact quality of life.
